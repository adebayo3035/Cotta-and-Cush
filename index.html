About Us

Cotta & Cush Limited (CCL) is a boutique product development company based in Lagos. Our specific areas of expertise are in web, mobile and data engineering with a track record in financial services, retail, e-commerce and logistics verticals.

Website Application Development

Start- Finish

Using the latest technologies we have developed a number of high performant web applications, some of which are an account opening application for a leading bank, a fundraising portal for a company in financial services and a freelancing marketplace for a design company.

Mobile Application Development

Start- Finish

We have developed mobile applications for field agents and customers of two leading insurance companies. We also developed an advert-funded internet service app for an Internet Service Provider.

Data Engineering

Full Throttle

We developed a tool for a leading bank to optimise its branch network by using location data, population density and economic activity data.
